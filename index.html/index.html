About the Album

"Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino" is the sixth studio album by the Arctic Monkeys, released in 2018. The album features a unique and cinematic style, with lyrics exploring various themes related to technology, politics, and society.

Explore the sonic landscapes and thought-provoking lyrics that make this album a standout in the Arctic Monkeys' discography.
